About Me

Teaching machines to reason, while saving curiosity for life

As an AI researcher, I am fascinated by how code and mathematics can help machines interpret a complex world. Whether it is enabling models to learn from unknown domains or generate detailed imagery, I enjoy the clarity and challenge that come with solving hard problems.

My research centers on Open-vocabulary Object Detection and Diffusion Models. I care deeply about model efficiency under limited resources, and that mindset also shapes how I build reliable environments with Docker and Linux.

"Observation is where understanding begins, and AI is one of the tools I use to extend that observation."

Outside research, I enjoy the freedom of open-world games and the craftsmanship of collectible figures. My interest in tarot symbolism also gives me a different, more intuitive perspective when reasoning about complex relationships in data.
